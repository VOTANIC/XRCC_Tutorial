Quick Start

Follow the steps for a quick start:

  1. Open the application to enter the Start Menu.

  1. Click "Create New".

  1. Enter a name for the project.

  1. Select Start Scene and choose "Cartoon Village".

  1. Click "Create"

  1. Select "Play Mode" ().

note

The robot at the center of the scene will be "you" the player who experiences the scene in first-person perspective.

  1. Press WASD to navigate the scene. Use mouse to rotate the view of user. Press Space bar to jump.

  2. Press ESC to stop the Play Mode and return to the Workspace.
