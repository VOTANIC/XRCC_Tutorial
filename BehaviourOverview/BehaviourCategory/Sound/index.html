Sound

Block NameBlockDescription
Play Sound

Play the specified audio clip asset.

  • Clip: Audio clip asset.
  • Volume: Number value specifying the audio play volume.
  • Loop: Boolean value specifying the audio play will be looped.
  • Spatial: Boolean value specifying if the 3D audio effect is enabled.
Pause Sound
Pause the audio clip playing.
Stop Sound
Stop the audio clip playing and reset to start of audio clip
Unpause Sound
Unpause the paused audio clip.
